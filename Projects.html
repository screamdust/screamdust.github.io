Story In My Eyes

"Story In My Eyes" project started as a part of initiative to raise awareness about intolerance with the intention to produce a change. At the beggining project was founded by the group of ambicious students that participated in P2PChallenge that aims to push back on hate, violence and extremism. As the part of the project the group had successfully held panel discussion, organized social experiment and conducted research on attitudes of LCC students.

Initiative for Dialogue and Conflict Transformation

"Initiative for Dialogue and Conflict Transformation" started to operate as an NGO beggining this year. The Initiative aims to equip people with knowledge on how productively resolve the conflicts as well as provide neccessary information to prevent the conflicts. The multicultural team of IFDACT has organized 2 events so far and is looking forward to organize event beyond local community.
